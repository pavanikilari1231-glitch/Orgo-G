About...

Red gram, commonly known as pigeon pea or toor dal, is a perennial legume that's grown as an annual crop. It's an important pulse crop cultivated for its edible seeds. The plant is a small shrub with trifoliate leaves and yellow, red, or pink flowers. The seeds, which come in various shades of red, brown, or yellow, are rich in protein and used in cooking across many cultures. Red gram is known for its ability to tolerate dry and semi-arid conditions, making it a crucial crop in regions with erratic rainfall patterns. It's also beneficial in crop rotation due to its nitrogen-fixing properties, enhancing soil fertility.

diseases & its fertilizer

Fusarium Wilt (Tuduk disease) symptoms:
 This is a fungal disease caused by Fusarium udum. It affects the vascular system of the plant, leading to wilting, yellowing, and eventual death of the plant.
Fusarium Wilt (Tuduk disease) solution:
 ⮞ Neem Cake: It contains natural nematocidal properties and can suppress soil-borne pathogens like Fusarium. Mix neem cake into the soil before planting.



Sterility Mosaic Disease (SMD) Symptoms:
 Sterility Mosaic Disease is mainly Caused by a virus transmitted by a mite, this disease leads to yellowing and curling of leaves, reduced flowering, and stunted growth.
Sterility Mosaic Disease (SMD) Solution:
 ⮞ Panchagavya: A fermented organic concoction made from cow dung, cow urine, milk, curd, and ghee. It enhances plant immunity and overall health.



Pod Blight Symptom:
 It is caused by a fungus and affects the pods, resulting in the development of dark-colored spots or lesions on the pods. It can lead to reduced yield and quality.
Pod Blight Solution:
 ⮞Trichoderma: Use Trichoderma-enriched organic formulations. Trichoderma species are known to antagonize many soil-borne pathogens, including those causing pod blight and phytophthora blight.



leaf spot disease symptom:
 Various fungal species can cause leaf spot diseases, resulting in the appearance of small, dark spots on the leaves. Severe infections can lead to defoliation and reduced photosynthesis
leaf spot disease Solution:
 ⮞ Biocontrol Agents: Utilize biocontrol agents like Bacillus subtilis or Pseudomonas fluorescens. They can be formulated into organic sprays to combat various leaf spot fungi.
